# Use fake cards

At Kapten, we have chosen Adyen as our Payment Service Provider (PSP). 

On the testing environments, there is no need to use your real credit card.

Instead, visit this link https://docs.adyen.com/development-resources/test-cards/test-card-numbers

And chose the desired credit card for your test.

## Some examples

1. Visa 4035 5010 0000 0008 03/2030 737
2. Visa 4977 9494 9494 9497 03/2030 737
3. Mastercard 5136 3333 3333 3335 03/2030 737
4. Mastercard 5555 4444 3333 1111 03/2030 737
